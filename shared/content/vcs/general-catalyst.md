General Catalyst is a multi-stage venture capital firm with $25+ billion in assets under management. Founded in 2000, the firm invests across AI, healthcare, fintech, and consumer technology with a focus on transformative companies.

General Catalyst has been particularly active in AI-powered healthcare solutions, backing companies like Suno AI (music generation), Curai Health (AI diagnostics), and Livongo (digital health). The firm takes a hands-on approach, often taking board seats and providing strategic guidance.

Known for backing companies through multiple rounds from seed to IPO, General Catalyst has helped build category-defining businesses including Stripe, Airbnb, and Snap. Their healthcare investing team includes former hospital executives and healthcare operators.
