Andreessen Horowitz (a16z) is a Silicon Valley venture capital firm founded in 2009 by Marc Andreessen and Ben Horowitz. The firm manages $35+ billion across multiple specialized funds including AI, crypto, bio, and games.

a16z launched a dedicated $1.25B AI fund and has been aggressive in backing generative AI startups. Their AI portfolio includes Character.AI, Mistral AI, Harvey (legal AI), and Adept. The firm is known for their thesis-driven investing and extensive operational support.

They provide portfolio companies with access to a 200+ person operations team covering recruiting, marketing, corporate development, and regulatory affairs. a16z publishes influential research on AI trends and hosts major industry events.
