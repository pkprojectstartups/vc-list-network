Sequoia Capital is one of the world's most prestigious venture capital firms, with a 50+ year track record of backing transformative companies. Known for early investments in Apple, Google, and PayPal, Sequoia continues to lead in AI and enterprise technology.

The firm manages over $85 billion in assets and has dedicated funds for AI, focusing heavily on LLM infrastructure, generative AI applications, and enterprise automation. Recent investments include OpenAI, Anthropic, and Databricks.

Sequoia is highly selective, partnering with fewer than 20 new companies per year globally. They offer extensive portfolio support including recruiting, business development, and technical guidance.
